This post demonstrates how to use images and tags in your blog posts.

## About Images

When adding images to your blog, you can:

1. Place them in the `static/` directory
2. Reference them using standard Markdown syntax

```markdown
![Alt text](/images/your-image.jpg)
```

## Using Tags

Tags help organize your content. This post has the following tags:
- `sample`
- `images`
- `blogging`

Readers can click on tags to find related content.

## Categories

Categories provide another way to group your posts. This post is in the "General" category.

## Writing Tips

Here are some tips for writing great blog posts:

- Use descriptive titles
- Break up content with headings
- Include relevant images
- Add tags and categories
- Write in a clear, engaging style

## Engaging Your Readers

The key to a successful blog is creating content that resonates with your audience. Share your knowledge, experiences, and unique perspective.
